THUNDER BOT

About Thunder Bot

Thunder Bot is a towering, lightning-powered robot with bold yellow and black stripes, glowing eyes, and a heart as big as his steel frame. Brought to life by a freak lightning storm in a scientist’s lab, he’s strong, curious, and always ready to help. Though his appearance is powerful and imposing, Thunder Bot is gentle and kind, especially with kids. He doesn’t speak, but he communicates through expressive gestures, protective actions, and heroic deeds that show he’s more than just a machine—he’s a true friend and guardian to the town.
